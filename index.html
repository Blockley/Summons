Agenda

  1. To open the Lodge.
  2. To take as read and consider for confirmation the minutes of the Regular Meeting held on 18 November 2023.
  3. To invest those Officers who were not present at the Installation Meeting.
  4. To pass Bro. Alex Price, initiated on 26th March 2024.
  5. To elect a Master and Treasurer for the ensuing year. The only nominations of which the Secretary is aware are Bro Alexander Humphries as Master and W Bro Matthew Standage as Treasurer, and subject to the provisions of Rules 105(a) and 112(a) respectively, it is proposed to declare the ballot in their favour. Any member may, however, demand a ballot.
  6. To ballot for the exclusion of Bros. Yasin, Richardson, Sisokin and Davis for non-payment of annual subscriptions in accordance with the Lodge bylaws and Rule 181 BoC.
  7. To recieve reports
  8. Notice of propositions.
  9. To close the Lodge.

Dress: dark or morning suit, undress reglia

The Lodge welcome enquiries for Membership.

“Be very cautious, whom you recommend as a candidate for Membership; one false step on this point may be fatal. If you introduce a disputatious person, confusion will be produced which may end in the dissolution of the Lodge. If you have a good Lodge keep it select. Great numbers are not always beneficial.” – Dr Oliver

Any Brother who is interested in pursuing Exaltation in the Holy Royal Arch, for which the qualification is to have been a Master Mason for at least four weeks, is invited to contact the Royal Arch Representative.
